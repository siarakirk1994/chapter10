Piano Lessons

We offer piano lessons for all ages, beginning at three. Whether you're a beginner or have been playing piano for awhile, we have a program to suit your needs. Learn new techniques, theory, harmony, and tone. Learning how to play the piano helps develop hand-eye coordination and self-confidence.

Guitar Lessons

We teach acoustic guitar, electric guitar, bass guitar, and ukuele. We have guitar lessons for beginners, intermediate platers, and advanced players. Improve your skills with weekly lessons. Learn new tips and techniques from experienced instructors. We also have a rock band program that you can join!

Violin Lessons

We offer violin lessons for all ages, beginning at age six. Our violin lessons include a variety of music styles, from fiddle to classical. Our instructors will help you fine-tune techniques and accomplish your muscial goals. We work with you to create a customized program, tailored to your needs.
